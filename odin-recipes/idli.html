IDLI RECIPE

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black gram or Urad daal.
  • Baking soda.
  • Salt.
  • Kitchen Oil.

Steps

  1. Blend rice and black gram together until they have a paste like texter.
  2. Now add a little bit of baking soda and salt.
  3. Put the paste in fridge for a night.
  4. Now apply a little bit of kitchen oil to the idli maker.
  5. Put the paste in idli maker and put it on gas.
  6. Wait for about 10 to 15 min and your idli is ready.
