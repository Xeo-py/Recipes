Tea Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for an Indian style tea.

Ingredients

  • Milk.
  • Tea leafs.
  • Suger (optional).
  • Turmeric.

Steps

  1. Boil the milk.
  2. Add tea leafs.
  3. Add suger acording to your taste.
  4. Add turmuric for smell and taste.
  5. Wait till tea is boiled.
  6. Your tea is ready.
